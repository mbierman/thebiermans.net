Directions to Michael & Laurie's House (Google Maps)

Our house is near 101 and 87 (on the opposite side of HWY 101 from San Jose International Airport.

From 101 South-bound (including San Francisco International Airport)

  1. Take 101 to MONTAGUE EXPRESSWAY (the freeway ramp forks. the first exit is SAN TOMAS EXPRESSWAY, MONTAGUE is the second ramp.
  2. You will loop over HWY 101 and see Intel on your left hand side.
  3. Go down two traffic lights to DE LA  CRUZ and turn RIGHT.
  4. Make a LEFT almost immediately on OAKWOOD DRIVE.
  5. Turn RIGHT on EDGEFIELD DRIVE.
  6. Our house will be on the right side at the corner.

From 101 North-bound

  1. Get off the first exit before HWY 87, it will read, "Trimble Road/De La Cruz Exit 391"
  2. Turn LEFT onto De la Cruz.
  3. Make the FIRST LEFT on to De La Cruz. (Yes, De La Cruz makes a 90 degree turn here; strange, but true.) This turn is IMMEDIATELY after you exit the freeway, so get over quickly.
  4. Go down to GREENWOOD DRIVE (the THIRD traffic light) and turn RIGHT on and go East for ~500 feet.
  5. Make the FIRST LEFT on BROWNWOOD WAY and go North for ~400 feet.
  6. Make the FIRST RIGHT on EDGEFIELD DRIVE and go East for ~1/2 mile. In front of you, you will see a fork in the road. The house at the tip of fork is ours.

From San Jose International Airport

  1. Take HWY 87 N to HWY101 N.
  2. As HWY 87 merges with 101 stay to the right and look for the sign that reads, "Trimble Road/De La Cruz Exit 391"
  3. Make the FIRST LEFT on to De La Cruz. (Yes, De La Cruz makes a 90 degree turn here. Strange, but true.) This turn is IMMEDIATELY after you exit the freeway, so get over quickly.
  4. Go down to GREENWOOD DRIVE (the THIRD traffic light) and turn RIGHT on and go East for ~500 feet.
  5. Make the FIRST LEFT on BROWNWOOD WAY and go North for ~400 feet.
  6. Make the FIRST RIGHT on EDGEFIELD DRIVE and go East for ~1/2 mile. In front of you, you will see a fork in the road. The house at the tip of fork is ours.
