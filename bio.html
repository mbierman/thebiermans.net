Bio

Michael Bierman is lead product manager for Web Tools at Nokia. Bierman has many years of experience in native, embeded, and web software development tools and web technologies. He was the senior product manager for Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) at Adobe, where he launched SVG within Adobe and served as product manager to the SVG Viewer and to Adobe products. Before joining Adobe, Bierman was at Sun Microsystems with the Java marketing team, and spent two years as a product manager at Microtec/Mentor Graphics working on compilers for embedded systems. Bierman also used to be Senior Product Manager at eBay.
